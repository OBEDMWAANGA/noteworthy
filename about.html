Welcome To Our School

NOTEWORTHY STEM SCHOOL

At NOTEWORTHY, we are dedicated to providing a dynamic and immersive learning experience that prepares students for success in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Our innovative curriculum integrates hands-on learning experiences, cutting-edge technology, and real-world applications to inspire a lifelong love of learning.

Services We Provide

At Noteworthy School, we offer a range of services designed to provide students with a comprehensive and enriching educational experience.

  • Nursery Program
  • Grade 1-4 Classes
  • Coding and Robotics Classes
  • Little Chief Cooking Classes

Nursery

Our Nursery Program provides a nurturing and stimulating environment for young learners to embark on their educational journey. Through play-based activities, social interaction, and hands-on learning experiences, our experienced educators foster the development of essential skills.

Enroll

Grade 1-4

Our Grade 1-4 classes provide a solid foundation in core academic subjects while also integrating STEM concepts into the curriculum. Our dedicated teachers support students as they develop essential literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills.

Enroll

Coding and Robotics

Our specialized coding and robotics programs offer students the opportunity to explore the exciting world of technology and engineering. Through interactive projects, competitions, and coding challenges, students learn valuable coding skills and develop proficiency in robotics concepts.

Enroll

Little Chief Cooking Class

We offer Little Chief Cooking classes to introduce students to cooking skills and healthy eating habits. In these hands-on cooking classes, students learn cooking techniques and create delicious and nutritious dishes.
